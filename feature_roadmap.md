# Gladiator Goodness - Feature Roadmap

**Version:** 0.1 (Draft)  
**Date:** July 17, 2026  
**Goal:** Deliver a focused, polished MVP while leaving room for a strong sequel.

## Base Game (MVP - v1.0)
Focused on the core fantasy: Build and manage a gladiator guild, fight in spectacular arena battles, and turn losses into powerful legacies via essences.

### Core Systems
- **Guild Hub / Roster Management**
  - Recruit, view, train, and manage gladiators and monsters.
  - Basic training tied to primary stats (Vitality, Endurance, etc.).

- **Market Tab**
  - Generic marketplace for purchasing gladiators, monsters, and equipment.
  - Simple economy (gold from arena wins/reputation).

- **Arena Combat**
  - Fully automated battles with personality-driven stances and Adaptation mechanic.
  - Stats system (primaries + secondaries) as documented.
  - Strong VFX/audio feedback for stances, crits, dodges, and crowd moments.

- **Essence & Progression**
  - On death: Harvest essences from fallen gladiators.
  - Forge signature weapons with unique abilities (straightforward crafting).
  - Permanent guild growth through powerful gear and reputation.

- **Crowd Satisfaction**
  - Hype meter driven by big plays and personality.
  - Influences rewards, reputation, and selective combat buffs.

- **Roguelike-lite Structure**
  - Runs with increasing difficulty and special events.
  - Multiple arena matches per run.

- **Polish & Presentation**
  - Intuitive UI for stats, combat log, roster.
  - Solid art, animations, and audio.
  - Basic saving and progression.

**Target Scope:** Tight, replayable experience emphasizing roster-building, tactical auto-battles, and emergent drama from personalities.

## Potential Sequel Features (Deferred)
These are exciting but would significantly expand scope. Save for "Gladiator Goodness: [Sequel Title]".

- Deep camp simulation (relationships, daily life, prostitutes, etc.)
- Character castes and "Road to Freedom" narrative arcs
- Advanced multi-step weapon crafting with specialist NPCs
- Recruiting and managing support staff (blacksmiths, monster hunters, trainers)
- Complex monster sourcing expeditions and training battles
- Expanded story/campaign with multiple endings
- Deeper economy and guild management systems
- Additional arena modes, tournaments, rival guilds

---

This keeps v1.0 focused and achievable while setting up a strong foundation for future content. Adjust priorities based on playtesting feedback.
